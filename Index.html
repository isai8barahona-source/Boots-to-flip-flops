About Isai

The same discipline that made us warriors can make us whole.

Isai Barahona is a United States Marine Corps veteran — Sergeant, 9 years active duty, combat deployments. After nearly a decade in uniform, he walked the same road so many veterans know: the loss of structure, the search for identity, the quiet struggle of rebuilding a life without a mission.

That journey didn't break him. It became his calling.

Over 11 years as a hands-on healer, Isai developed a deep understanding of how the body holds stress, trauma, and tension. In Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy, we speak of fluid bodies, fluid tides, and profound stillness — the same stillness available to every veteran willing to go beneath the surface.

He brings what no textbook can teach: the hands, the heart, and the hard-won wisdom of someone who has walked through fire and chosen to help others find their way through.

Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy
You remain fully clothed on a massage table. Using an incredibly gentle touch — no more than the weight of a nickel — BCST works with your nervous system's own rhythms to release stored tension and support deep regulation. No manipulation, no force. The body does the work.
Addresses trauma held in the body that talk therapy cannot reach
Supports nervous system shift from fight-or-flight to rest
Particularly effective for TBI, concussion, and blast-related symptoms
No clothing removal required — always fully clothed

The Boots to Flip-Flops Mission
In Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy, we speak of fluid tides and deep stillness — the body's innate intelligence moving beneath the surface. Boots to Flip-Flops is built on this same principle: beneath the noise of transition, beneath the weight of service, there is a stillness in every veteran waiting to be found.

This is a secular, peer-led, body-centered space. No dogma. No judgment. Just the work.
