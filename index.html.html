About Isai

The same discipline that made us warriors can make us whole.

Isai Barahona is a United States Marine Corps veteran — Sergeant, 9 years active duty, combat deployments. After nearly a decade in uniform, he walked the same road so many veterans know: the loss of structure, the search for identity, the quiet struggle of rebuilding a life without a mission.

That journey didn't break him. It became his calling.

Over 11 years as a hands-on healer, Isai developed a deep understanding of how the body holds stress, trauma, and tension. That work evolved into advanced training in Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy — and a commitment to creating spaces where veterans can exhale and begin the journey back to themselves.

He brings what no textbook can teach: the hands, the heart, and the hard-won wisdom of someone who has walked through fire and chosen to help others find their way through.

What I Offer

Every veteran's path is different. These offerings meet you where you are — whether you're just beginning to take off the boots, or already walking barefoot.

01
One-on-One Sessions
Private peer support combining breathwork, nervous system regulation, somatic awareness, and honest conversation. For veterans navigating identity, transition, PTSD, panic, and purpose.
02
Group Wellness Events
Facilitated group experiences for veterans — breathwork, meditation, visualization, and emotional regulation in a safe, secular, judgment-free setting. Community as medicine.
03
Somatic Healing
Hands-on healing through massage therapy and Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy — addressing how the body stores trauma and tension, and supporting the nervous system's return to balance.

The Boots to Flip-Flops Mission
